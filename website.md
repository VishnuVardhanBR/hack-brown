# Metropolis
## AI-Powered Event Discovery & Itinerary Planner

**Discover events. Build your perfect day.**

Metropolis is a cutting-edge mobile application that leverages **autonomous AI agents** to discover events and intelligently build personalized itineraries for you. Whether you're a local looking for something to do or a traveler exploring a new city, Metropolis curates the perfect schedule based on your budget, schedule, and interests.
